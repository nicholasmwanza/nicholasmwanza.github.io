CLINICAL TRIALS

This project presents an AI-assisted adaptive clinical trial framework designed to identify and respond to early treatment failure in patients with Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus (T2DM). The study integrates machine learning based risk prediction, adaptive trial design, and real world data simulation to improve personalized treatment decision making and trial efficiency. Traditional clinical trials in diabetes often rely on static treatment allocation and population level outcomes. This project demonstrates how artificial intelligence can be embedded within an adaptive trial framework to dynamically stratify risk, guide treatment adjustments, and compare outcomes between conventional and AI-adaptive trial arms..
